The Best Movies According to Esther

My top 3 movies of all-time.

When the Life Gives You Tangerines

Its heartwarming storytelling, stunning Jeju Island backdrop, and beautiful portrayal of the bittersweet journey of youth and self-discovery make it a compelling watch.

Princess Mononoke

Its breathtaking animation, powerful environmental message, and masterful portrayal of the complex balance between nature and humanity make it a must-watch.

Pilot

Its unique blend of humor and heart tells an inspiring story of reinvention and resilience, highlighted by a standout performance from Jo Jung-suk.
